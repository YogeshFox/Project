Wifi_Status:

The ESP8266 microchip offers built-in Wi-Fi support, enabling effortless connection to 802.11 b/g/n networks. Whether acting as a station or access point, it facilitates seamless data transfer over Wi-Fi, making it perfect for IoT projects requiring remote control and communication.

Battery_Status:

Voltage sensors play a pivotal role in battery management, monitoring voltage levels to assess both the state of charge and health. This data is essential for optimizing battery performance, preventing over-discharge, and prolonging battery life across various applications.

Relay_status:

The provided relay configurations allow for versatile functionality in managing battery operations: from displaying battery voltage during charging to indicating discharge conditions with varying amperage levels, facilitating efficient monitoring and control of battery status in diverse scenarios.

Current_Mode:

These modes offer comprehensive control over battery operations, enabling precise monitoring and management: from distinguishing between high and low amperage during discharge to displaying battery voltage during charging, ensuring efficient utilization and maintenance of battery systems across various applications.
